# Metrics and Tests Checklist

- Is the primary metric appropriate for the task and class balance?
- Are per-class or subgroup metrics needed?
- Are confidence intervals, bootstrap estimates, or permutation tests required?
- Is calibration needed in addition to ranking or accuracy metrics?
- Could threshold choice change the conclusion?
- Does the reported aggregate hide important failure modes?
- Are multiple comparisons or repeated sweeps being over-interpreted?
