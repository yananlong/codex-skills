# Section Archetypes

- **Empirical paper**: lead with problem, method, main evidence, then analysis and limitations.
- **Method paper**: emphasize mechanism, strongest comparison, and minimal ablations that isolate the claimed factor.
- **Benchmark or artifact paper**: front-load why the artifact matters, what is genuinely new, and how evaluation avoids triviality.
- **Finding paper**: keep the methodological novelty modest and put more weight on the empirical result and its caveats.
