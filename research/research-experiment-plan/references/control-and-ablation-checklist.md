# Control and Ablation Checklist

- Is the strongest relevant baseline included rather than a padded weak list?
- Does each ablation isolate one factor rather than several bundled changes?
- Are data, compute, training budgets, and model capacity comparable?
- Is the evaluation protocol identical across compared systems?
- Are the controls strong enough to falsify the claim instead of just decorate it?
- Which experiments are required before more scaling or tuning makes sense?
- What can be deferred without weakening the decision?
- Would a skeptical reviewer call any comparison unfair or non-decisive?
