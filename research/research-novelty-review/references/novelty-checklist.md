# Novelty Checklist

- What exactly is claimed to be new?
- Which part is the strongest contribution: framing, method, protocol, artifact, or result?
- Which prior works are closest, not just vaguely related?
- Would a skeptical reviewer call this recombination, renaming, or routine extension?
- Can the claim be narrowed to a defensible first, clearest comparison, or highest-value framing?
- If the novelty is modest, what is the strongest usefulness claim instead?
