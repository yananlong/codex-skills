# Novelty Decision Scale

Use a 1-5 decision scale instead of a coarse `ABANDON / REFRAME / PROCEED` label.

- `1`: abandon; closest overlaps or routine-combination effects kill the core claim.
- `2`: major reframe required; some useful work may remain, but not under the current novelty story.
- `3`: narrow proceed only; the work may still be worth doing, but only with sharply reduced positioning.
- `4`: proceed with careful positioning; novelty looks real but is still vulnerable to skeptical overlap arguments.
- `5`: strong novelty position; the closest overlaps do not materially undercut the core contribution.

Use a separate 1-5 confidence rating to show how stable the decision is under the available search coverage.
