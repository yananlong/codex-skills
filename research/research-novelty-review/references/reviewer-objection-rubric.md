# Reviewer Objection Rubric

- "This is a renamed known pattern."
- "This is a routine extension or scaling result."
- "The benchmark or evaluation protocol is the only thing that changed."
- "The method is a standard bundle rather than a novel mechanism."
- "The empirical finding may be useful, but the methodological novelty is weak."
- "The paper should be reframed as value or synthesis, not as major novelty."
