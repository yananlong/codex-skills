# TabMol DDI/OOD Adapter

For the current DDI/OOD agenda, novelty should be tested against the objection that the work is only:

- scaffold plus temporal plus inductive splits with better packaging
- a DDI benchmark paper rather than a general pairwise OOD framework
- a result about DrugBank missingness rather than robustness

Pressure-test whether the real novelty is:

- an explicit pairwise OOD taxonomy
- a split and leakage audit spec
- shift-severity quantification
- a reporting and statistical testing standard
- a reusable framework that happens to be instantiated on DDI
