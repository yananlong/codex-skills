# Overlap Scoring Rubric

- `1`: almost no meaningful overlap; prior work is only distantly adjacent.
- `2`: mild overlap in framing or problem area, but not in the decisive mechanism or protocol.
- `3`: meaningful overlap that weakens the claim and forces narrow positioning.
- `4`: strong overlap that likely forces a major reframe.
- `5`: kill-shot overlap; the claimed novelty is effectively already present.
- Default upward when the contribution differs mainly in naming, packaging, scale, or benchmark choice.
