# Adversarial Literature Checklist

Use this checklist to red-team a systematic synthesis before finalizing conclusions.

## Claim integrity

- Rewrite major claims in falsifiable form.
- Flag overgeneralization beyond studied populations or contexts.
- Flag endpoint switching between methods and conclusions.

## Method integrity

- Check whether study designs support causal language.
- Check confounding control adequacy.
- Check baseline/comparator fairness.
- Check whether missing data handling is reported.

## Evidence integrity

- Check whether high-risk-of-bias studies dominate conclusions.
- Check whether null/negative studies are underweighted.
- Check for citation laundering.
- Check for unsupported "state-of-the-art" assertions.

## Statistical and practical significance

- Distinguish statistical from practical significance.
- Check effect-size interpretation consistency.
- Check sensitivity to outliers or subgroup slicing.

## External validity and failure modes

- Probe domain shift risks.
- Probe deployment or real-world transfer assumptions.
- Identify plausible failure modes ignored by the synthesis.

## Final red-team output

- List critical conclusion-changing issues first.
- Provide targeted rewrite suggestions.
- Mark unverified claims and required evidence.
